# 任意密码重置

![img](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/60657001/1779965207129-3c83afcd-35ed-4b3f-9400-5e58e167735f.png)

![img](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/60657001/1779965207427-e5c43ca0-7e18-44c8-a72d-4b3713e4a892.png)
